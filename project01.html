03 - Definition & Ideation

    USER INSIGHT:

    Pet owners need a vet service that is both affordable and flexible with their schedule, because users can’t get affordable vet care if they can’t be certain they’ll receive a vet visit.

    PROBLEM STATEMENT:

    Emancipet is a nonprofit that provides free and affordable vet care. We’ve observed that users aren’t able to confirm an appointment with Emancipet ahead of time, which is causing users to seek other vet services where making an appointment in advance is possible. How might we improve Emancipet’s website so that pet owners can successfully schedule a visit ahead of time, based on an increase in the number of pet owners visiting the website and creating an account?

FEATURE PRIORITIZATION MATRIX

    Based on our problem statement, we then put together a feature prioritization matrix using the "I like, I wish, what if" method and dot-voted on our favorite features.

04 - Prototyping

PROTOTYPE SKETCHES

    We began our prototyping phase with rough sketches of the home page, services page, pet records page, and new pages for the patient portal and scheduling system we want to add to the website.

LO-FI PROTOTYPE

    We took our sketches and created a rough prototype. This lo-fi prototype was then user tested, and the feedback we were given helped us iterate our mid and hi-fi prototypes.

MID-FI PROTOTYPE

    We created our black-and-white, mid-fi prototype to be stylistically faithful to Emancipet's current site design.
